Patrick McGuire

Fourth-year mechanical engineering student at Northeastern University

About Me

I am interested in robotics and aerospace engineering. I have experience in mechanical design, electrical design, and programming. I am passionate about hands-on work and solving complex problems. I enjoy projects where mechanical design is integrated with electronics and software.

Five Summer Internships at Woods Hole Oceanographic Institution

I developed underwater robots, including mechanical and circuit board design. I wrote the entire control software for an autonomous robot. I conducted field trials locally and in the U.S. Virgin Islands.

  • TideRider Water Quality Robot

  • CUREE Coral Reef Monitoring Robot

Robotics Co-op at Apeiron Labs

I worked on the mechanical design for Apeiron Lab's robot and supporting systems intended for low-cost ocean monitoring solutions.   

  • Autonomous Vehicle Deployment System 

  • Hydraulic Pump Characterization System 

  • Development for Robotic Glider

Robotics Co-op at Fleet Robotics

I worked on Fleet's docking and charging station, as well as an accelerated wear testing setup. This included FEA, mechanical design and electrical system design.

  • Docking Station Development

  • Robotic Arm Wear-Test Rig

AerospaceNU Club at Northeastern

I am the club's treasurer, as well as the team lead for rocketry electronics and software. I competed in NASA's University Student Launch Initiative.

  • NASA Competition

  • High Power Rocketry Certification and Avionics

FIRST Robotics Competition Team 125

On FIRST Robotics Team 125 I served as team captain, mechanical team lead, and scouting team lead. I led the overall design of the team's robots from 2020-2022.  I designed 9 subsystems over my 8 years on the team.

  • FIRST Robotics Competition Mechanical Design

  • Scouting Software
